Booking Period: {{ booking_open_data.opens_for_booking_on|date:"jS F" }} to {{ booking_open_data.closes_for_initial_period_on|date:"jS F" }}

  • During this period, bookings are not "first come, first served"
  • Places may be booked at any point during the period
  • All information entered at the time of booking must be accurate
  • No deposit is required during the booking phase
  • If you have special requirements (like children with special needs), please ensure you book early in this period, by {{ booking_open_data.closes_for_initial_period_special_needs_on|date:"jS F" }} at the latest, to ensure there is time for the place to be approved

Oversubscribed Camps

If a camp is oversubscribed after the booking period closes, places will be allocated automatically using the following priority order:

  1. Children of CCiW leaders or officers currently serving
  2. Returning campers (prioritised by number of years previously attended)
  3. Campers who were on the waiting list last year and not offered a place
  4. Campers with a sibling attending the same camp
  5. Remaining places allocated by lottery, subject to availability

Multiple Camp Applications

If a camper is given a place on one camp, any other applications will be treated as lowest priority. CCiW cannot guarantee which place will be allocated first.
