You can book and pay online, providing you have a debit card, credit card, or PayPal account.

{% if booking_open_data.opens_for_booking_on %}

Due to very high demand, we cannot guarantee a place to everyone. Our system is as follows:

  • Bookings will be open from {{ booking_open_data.opens_for_booking_on|date:"j F" }}.
  • The initial booking period will finish on {{ booking_open_data.closes_for_initial_period|date:"j F" }}.
  • All bookings made during this period will then be considered together.
  • A priority system will be applied for some places, based on previous attendance and some other factors.
  • Any remaining places will be decided by a lottery system.
  • At this point we will notify you of the outcome.
  • Those who have not been given places will automatically be placed on a waiting list, in which case you may be offered a place if other bookings are cancelled.
  • Bookings after the initial period will still be accepted and added to the waiting list, but due to high demand they are unlikely to be offered places.

We're sorry that some people will inevitably be disappointed!

{% endif %}
