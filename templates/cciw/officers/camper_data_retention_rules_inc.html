Camper information data retention rules:
  • This data may be shared only with leaders and assistant leaders, and all such people must be aware of and abide by these rules.
  • If downloaded, the data must be stored securely only on devices that unauthorized people do not have access to. Data must be stored in an organized fashion that enables you to find and delete it later.
  • All camper information must be deleted from your electronic devices and online storage, including any copies you have made (such as attachments in emails and backups), within 1 month of the end of the camp they pertain to.
  • You may not share this information with any third parties.
